Jewel of Pakistan: Gilgit-Baltistan

By Asmar Hussain · 18 September 2023

“Autumnal Elegance of Hunza: A Photographic Tribute”

In the heart of Pakistan’s majestic mountain ranges, I found my sanctuary amidst the golden hues of Hunza Valley. For over a decade, I have returned year after year, with camera in hand, to capture the autumnal transformation that breathes life into this picturesque valley. Through my lens, I have witnessed the valley evolve, yet retain its magical allure, beckoning visitors to immerse themselves in its seasonal spectacle. As a fervent advocate for boosting autumn tourism for a decade, I invite you to journey with me through my experiences and rediscover Hunza Valley in its autumnal glory.

A Canvas Painted with Nature’s Brush

Over the years, my visits to the Hunza Valley have transformed from mere photographic expeditions to a deeply personal journey. Each autumn, the valley unveils a vibrant mosaic of gold, crimson, and amber hues, a canvas painted with nature’s brush. The lush greenery gracefully transitions, offering a stark contrast against the pristine mountains that stand as silent witnesses to the changing seasons. This transformation, both subtle and profound, has been my muse, inspiring me to capture moments that are both fleeting and eternal.

A Tapestry of Culture and Hospitality

My journey has been enriched by the warm embrace of the local communities, who have become more like family than hosts. Their hospitality, coupled with the rich cultural tapestry that weaves through the ancient forts of Baltit and Altit, has added depth and nuance to my photographic narrative. Through my lens, I have endeavored to capture not just the visual splendor, but also the spirit and heritage that pulsate through the valley.

Adventure and Tranquility: A Personal Odyssey

Each visit to the valley has been an odyssey of adventure and tranquility. From trekking the rugged trails of Eagle Nest to witnessing the tranquil beauty of the Mother of Mist Rakaposhi (7788m), my experiences have been both exhilarating and serene. The valley, with its serene landscape, has offered moments of peace, where nature and soul intertwine, creating a heaven for those seeking both adventure and relaxation.

Photographing Autumn’s Palette in Hunza Valley

Back in 2011, I found myself among a small group of tourists, mostly foreign visitors, enchanted by the autumn hues of Hunza Valley. The mesmerizing fall colors sparked a personal mission within me to promote autumn tourism in this picturesque haven.

Fast forward to a decade later, the valley has transformed into a popular destination during the fall, attracting not just photographers but a diverse group of tourists eager to witness its autumnal beauty. It fills me with great joy to see the increasing number of people journeying north to Hunza Valley. While my role in this transformation may be modest, the sight of more people basking in the valley’s autumn glow brings an unparalleled sense of fulfillment.

Your Guide to Experiencing Hunza Valley’s Fall Splendor

As the whispers of the approaching fall begin to rustle the leaves, I find myself compelled to share a little secret with fellow enthusiasts: if you truly wish to witness the pinnacle of autumn’s display, plan your journey to Hunza between the last week of October and the first week of November.

During this time, the valley transforms into a vibrant canvas, offering a visual feast that is nothing short of magical. To fully immerse yourself in this autumnal spectacle, make sure to visit some of the popular spots that have captivated me over the years. Wander through the serene gardens of Altit Fort, where history meets nature’s artistry. Ascend to the Eagle’s Nest for a bird’s eye view of Hunza, a vantage point that offers panoramic vistas of the valley adorned in fall colors.

Further, explore the hidden gems of Chamangul, Gulmit, and Passu, where the hues of autumn paint the landscapes with strokes of crimson and gold. Venture to the picturesque locales of Misgar, Morkhoon, and Jamalabad, where each turn unveils a new facet of autumn’s splendor.

As someone who has been enchanted by Hunza’s autumnal charm for over a decade, I can assure you that this journey will be a feast for your senses, a celebration of nature at its finest. I look forward to perhaps crossing paths with you in Hunza Valley, as we both seek to capture the fleeting yet eternal beauty of autumn through the Camera lens or the Eyes.

By [ASMAR HUSSAIN], an award winning landscape photographer
